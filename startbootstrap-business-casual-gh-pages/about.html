Little bout Me

I was born and raised in Colorado, as it turns out it did not help me avoid the attitude that my parents brought with them from Jersey. I grew up between Colorado and California. Durning school I learned my passion for the adrenaline sports. I began with quads and dirt bikes, moved in to snowboarding and wake boarding, followed by tracking my cars and bikes. As I began to adult I realized my pervious invincibility was beginning to show age. As such I had to pull back from racing and decided to move my attention to lower impact sports. So, after completing this program I will be getting my skydiving license as my grad present.

Ever since I was a kid I have enjoyed taking things apart to see how they worked. This led me to realize my love for working with my hands and building stuff, anything from circuit boards to houses. The ability to build a concept, was one of the leading reasons to join the coding world. Because I like many different industries I thought perfecting the ability to shift between them would be an ideal place to start.
